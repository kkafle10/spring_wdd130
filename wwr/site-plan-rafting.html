Purpose

The main goal of this website is to provide users with information on White River Rafting service that the business will provide. Along with that, details on price, location, business, and lodging will also be laid out. As the owner of the business, it is in the best interest of the business to provide accurate information of the business to the users before they decide on using the service or not. In short, the purpose of the website will be to generate revenue through raftimg, restaurant, and lodging services that will be provided along with useful information about the location for the users.

Audience

The targeted audience for this website is anyone from the age of 13-60 for rafting and people of all ages, background for lodging and fooding.
