    • Nepal

Nepal is a landlocked country located in South Asia. It is situated mainly in the Himalayas and bordered by China to the north and India to the east, south, and west. With a diverse geography that includes mountains, valleys, and plains, Nepal is known for its stunning landscapes and Mount Everest, the world's highest peak. The country is rich in cultural heritage, with a mix of Hindu and Buddhist traditions. Kathmandu, the capital city, is home to numerous temples, palaces, and historical sites. Nepal's economy relies heavily on agriculture, tourism, and remittances from Nepali workers abroad. Despite facing various challenges, including political transitions and natural disasters, Nepal remains a captivating destination for adventure seekers and cultural enthusiasts alike.
